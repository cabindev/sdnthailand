# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Thai general public (a wide age range, including older readers) who come to SDN Thailand (Sober Drivers / Sober Network Thailand) for articles, news, videos, and campaign activities about an alcohol-free lifestyle. Visitors arrive mostly from social media and search, often on mobile, to read a specific piece of content or learn about a campaign. A smaller set of authenticated users (members and admins) use a dashboard area for profiles, media requests, and procurement, but the public content hub is the primary surface.

## Product Purpose

SDN Thailand's public website is the organization's content and campaign hub for alcohol-free advocacy in Thailand. It publishes blog posts, news, and videos, and showcases awareness campaigns (Buddhist ordination, Dry January, PM 2.5, and others). Success looks like a credible, professional presence that makes the content easy to find and pleasant to read, builds trust in the organization, and motivates people toward a positive, alcohol-free life, without ever feeling preachy, dated, or cluttered.

## Brand Personality

Trustworthy and uplifting. Three words: credible, warm, professional. The voice is a hopeful public-health advocate, not a clinician and not a scold. It encourages a positive sober lifestyle through clear, well-organized content and a polished, modern look. Tone is supportive and confident; never fear-based, guilt-driven, or finger-wagging.

## Anti-references

- **Old Thai government portals**: dense, dated, bureaucratic, cramped layouts.
- **Cluttered, ad-heavy Thai news aggregators**: busy "everything everywhere" layouts that bury the content.
- **Fear-based PSA aesthetics**: graphic, guilt-driven anti-drinking shock imagery and an alarmist tone.
- Generic AI landing-page scaffolding: cream backgrounds, identical card grids, an uppercase eyebrow above every section.

## Design Principles

- **Content first, calm frame.** The article, news item, or video is the hero. Chrome (nav, footer, cards) stays quiet and gives content room to breathe.
- **Uplift, don't alarm.** Lead with a positive, hopeful vision of an alcohol-free life. Persuade through clarity and credibility, not fear.
- **Professional restraint.** Simple but refined. A polished, modern feel comes from spacing, hierarchy, and consistency, not decoration or density.
- **Built for everyone in Thailand.** Strong Thai-language readability across a wide age range, mobile-first, fast on modest connections.
- **One identity, consistently applied.** Keep the established brand (orange #ff7834 accent on near-black ink and clean white/dark surfaces, Seppuri headings + IBM Plex Sans Thai Looped body) coherent across every surface.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA: body text contrast ≥ 4.5:1 (large text ≥ 3:1), visible focus states, semantic structure, and labeled controls. Honor `prefers-reduced-motion` for all animations. Prioritize legible Thai typography and comfortable text sizes for a broad, all-ages audience. Light and dark themes both meet AA.
